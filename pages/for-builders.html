Common Questions

What builders ask us

Q

Do I need special training to install SIP panels?

No. We provide full installation guides and our technical team is available by phone during your build. Most experienced builders find the system intuitive after the first project.

Q

Can I use SIPs with my existing subcontractors?

Yes. SIP panels work within standard NZ building sequences. Electricians, plumbers, and plasterers all work with SIP structures regularly — it's just a different substrate.

Q

What if panels need modifying on site?

Panels can be cut on site with standard tools if needed. We design to minimise this, but our technical team can advise on site modifications if required.
