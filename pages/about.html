About Supersub

New Zealand's SIP panel specialists — made right here.

Supersub is a New Zealand-owned and operated SIP panel manufacturer based in Cambridge, Waikato. We exist to make high-performance construction more accessible, more efficient, and more affordable for builders, developers, and homeowners across NZ.

Our Mission

Making better building accessible for every New Zealander

We believe that high-performance building shouldn't be limited to premium projects. SIP panels make warm, structurally excellent homes achievable at a price that works — and we're working to make that technology accessible to builders and homeowners at every level.

With timber prices at record highs and building costs continuing to rise, we think the industry needs a smarter path forward. SIP panels are that path — and we're committed to making NZ a leader in this approach to construction.
