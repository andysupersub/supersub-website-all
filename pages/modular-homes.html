Your Journey

From consultation to move-in

Step 01

Pre-Consultation

We review your site, budget, and requirements. Site access, foundation type, and council requirements are all assessed.

Step 02

Design & Consent

Your home design is finalised and building consent is obtained. Our team manages the documentation process.

Step 03

Factory Build

Your home is built in our Cambridge facility — SIP panel structure, full fit-out, inspections at every stage.

Step 04

Delivery & Install

Your home is transported to site, set on piles or slab, and connected to services. Ready to move in.
