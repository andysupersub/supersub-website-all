SnapLock Walls

A faster-to-install wall system built on SIP panel technology. Designed for builders who need speed, repeatability, and consistent quality on every job — without the complexity of fully custom panel design.

The Concept

Speed and simplicity — without sacrificing performance

SnapLock Walls takes the performance of SIP panels and packages it into a standardised system that builders can install faster and with less coordination overhead. Pre-sized panels with integrated joining systems mean less time planning, less time on site.

Standardised panel sizes — less custom design time
Integrated SnapLock joining system — fast on-site assembly
Same thermal and structural performance as full SIP panels
Ideal for repeat floor plans — subdivision and volume builders
How It Works

Simpler than custom SIPs. Faster than timber.

01

Select your wall config

Choose from our standardised SnapLock panel range. Standard heights and widths cover most residential applications.

02

We cut & prep in factory

Panels are cut to your floor plan requirements in our Cambridge factory — openings, chases, and all joining surfaces prepared.

03

Snap together on site

Panels arrive numbered and sequenced. The SnapLock system means panels click together — minimal hardware, maximum speed.

Who It's For

Volume Home Builders

Building 10+ homes per year on similar floor plans? SnapLock Walls gives you a repeatable system you can train your crew to install consistently.

Subdivision Developers

Developing a subdivision with common layouts? Standardise on SnapLock and dramatically reduce your per-home build time and coordination overhead.

Transportable Builders
