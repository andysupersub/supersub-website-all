The Core Technology

What is a SIP Panel — and why does it matter?

A Structurally Insulated Panel (SIP) is a high-performance building component — engineered timber skins bonded to a rigid foam core. It replaces traditional timber framing with a single system that's faster, stronger, and more energy-efficient.

Up to 60% faster to construct than traditional timber framing
Exceeds NZ H1 thermal code — lower power bills for homeowners
Factory precision — consistent quality, minimal waste on site
Locally manufactured in Cambridge, Waikato
